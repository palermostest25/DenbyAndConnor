MS-DOS Mode Implementation for Windows 10 by Denby Daily
To Setup

Step 1 Open setup.bat.
Step 2 Follow instructions.
Step 3 Wait until it stops.
Step 4 Use WinXEditor to put MS-DOS Mode in the WinX menu.
Step 5 Right click the start button or press Win+X and click MS-DOS mode or the name that you called it.

WARNING:
Do not use this on a host machine, it is a proof of conecpt for now and doing so could damage your windows installation.
